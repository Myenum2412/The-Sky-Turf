Frequently Asked Questions

What type of footwear is allowed?

We allow all types of sports shoes including studs, turf shoes, and canvas shoes. Metal studs are not permitted to protect our artificial turf surface.

How far in advance can I book?

Bookings can be made up to 1 month in advance. We recommend booking at least 2 hours ahead for regular slots and 1-2 days for weekend bookings.

Is equipment provided?

Goals and nets are permanently installed. Cricket stumps and boundary markers are available. Players must bring their own balls and personal equipment.

What happens during rain?

Our artificial turf is playable during light rain. For heavy rain or safety concerns, we offer full refunds or rescheduling options.

Are there parking facilities?

Yes, we have secure parking space for both cars and two-wheelers with easy access to the ground.

Can we organise tournaments?

We provide event coordination support for tournaments and can arrange for extended bookings and additional facilities as needed.
